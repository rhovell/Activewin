Deposits need to be protected

If you have paid a deposit, either at the property you currently live in or at any property you had lived in since April 2012, then your landlord was required to register your deposit with one of the three government backed tenancy deposit schemes.

As well as registering the deposit, your landlord was also required to provide you with a certificate confirming the deposit had been registered; information known as the Prescribed Inforamtion that explains what can happen to the deposit; and a leaflet from the chosen scheme explaining how the tenancy deposit scheme works.

If this has not been complied with, then you may be entitled to compensation.
